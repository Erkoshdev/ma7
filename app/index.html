In business, people are paramount.
We believe that it's the brilliance of individuals that ignites
the success of a company.
This is why, through strategic venture investing and
providing essential resources, we commit to empowering
these visionaries, securing entrepreneurs' trust as they
navigate the future of their businesses.

I have spent my entire career in the tech business, with a focus on human capital. My experience has shown that the key to success is not finance, but people. This is why I am dedicated to helping entrepreneurs develop significant projects.

At MA7, we seek founders who are true innovators and game-changers, driven by an undiminishable passion. We invest in the people behind the great ideas.
